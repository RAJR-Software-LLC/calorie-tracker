# Privacy Policy (Production Draft)

Last updated: 2026-04-15

## Overview

Calorie Tracker ("we", "our", "us") provides nutrition and wellness tracking features. This policy explains
what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data We Collect

- Account data: email address and authentication identifiers.
- User-provided wellness data: meals, calorie entries, goals, and related notes.
- Technical data: app version, platform, and diagnostic error data needed for reliability.

## How We Use Data

- Provide core app functionality (authentication, syncing entries, goal progress).
- Protect security and prevent abuse.
- Troubleshoot failures and improve reliability.

## Health-Related Data Commitments

- We do not sell personal health-related data.
- We do not use health-related data for advertising profiling or marketing targeting.
- We only use health-related data to operate and improve wellness tracking features.

## Sharing

We may share data with:

- Infrastructure and service providers who process data on our behalf.
- Authorities when legally required.

We do not share personal health-related data with data brokers.

## Retention

We retain data while your account is active and as needed for legal, tax, fraud-prevention, and security
purposes. You may request deletion as described below.

## Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards. No method of storage or
transmission is completely secure.

## Your Choices and Rights

- Access and correction: request access or updates to your account data.
- Deletion: request account and data deletion.
- Consent withdrawal: where processing relies on consent, you may withdraw it.

## Account Deletion

Use the in-app account deletion path (when available) or follow the instructions at:

- `EXPO_PUBLIC_ACCOUNT_DELETION_URL`

## Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 (or higher age where required by local law).

## International Transfers

If data is processed outside your country, we apply safeguards required by applicable law.

## Contact

For privacy requests and questions, provide a support contact in your production policy page.

## Medical Disclaimer

Calorie Tracker is for general wellness and informational use only. It is not a medical device and does not
diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition.
