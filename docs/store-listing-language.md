## Full Description

Calorie Tracker helps you log meals, monitor calorie intake, and stay consistent with your nutrition goals.

Use simple tools to record food, estimate calorie needs, and review daily progress over time. The app is
designed for general wellness, healthy habits, and personal goal tracking.

Features include:

- Meal and calorie logging
- Daily progress summaries
- Calorie planning tools
- Family and shared tracking support

Important: This app is for general wellness and informational purposes only. It is not a medical device and
does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition. Always seek the advice of a qualified
healthcare professional for medical questions, diagnosis, or treatment decisions.
